I'm Sergiu Toderascu — I ship mobile apps and developer tooling from Chisinau, Moldova. I learn systems by taking them apart, then put them back together simpler.

About

My work moves between two worlds. On the mobile side, I build Capacitor plugins that bridge web apps to native iOS and iCloud, and native SwiftUI components engineered for performance at scale. On the Tooling side, I maintain debuno — bringing Deno-style module resolution to Bun and Node — with a family of RPC and error-handling libraries around it.

I've been on GitHub since 2011. The thread running through ninety-odd public repositories is the same: explore by building, then leave the tool behind for the next person.
